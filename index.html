Welcome to our Budget Tracking Website!

Not only does our system dynamically calculate your balance based on the incomes and expenses you input, but it also will show you how much of your budget is spent on each category of expenses so that you can see where your money really goes.

Instructions:

Simply enter the information in the boxes below and then press submit. You can submit as many incomes and expenses as you want. Watch the balance and the lists below update every time you press the submit button! If you would like to see only a certain category of your incomes or expenses, use the filter option just above the list to choose which you would like to see. You can enter the date you received the income/spent the money and filter by date, too. If you don't enter a date, it automatically shows todays date.
